The creativity of this event has to be spot on! Using only earbuds, the backs of your pencils, your fingers (aka things available to you literally, at your fingertips) and acrylic paints as a medium, create a masterpiece using dots instead of brush strokes. The theme will be revealed on the spot (see what we did there?), so make sure you rack your brains to incorporate it in your artwork!


Lines, often the unacknowledged pioneers of art are the reason for the most beautiful works ever created. It joins every point and keeps it connected. It creates order in chaos. Hence, lines!- the first thing that we ever drew is going to be the face of FA’s first-ever Digital Art Event too. Canva will be the software used. Each participant will be sent a link to a Canva page. The page will consist of 2-3 lines (geometrical segments). The lines must be copy-pasted (no limit), positioned and edited in such a way that the end product is an art piece relevant to the topic.


We’ve heard that empty vessels make noise but they also make beautiful images. Photography is about looking at things and finding beauty in the ordinary and mundane and that is what we expect our participants to do. Participants are expected to use empty utensil(s) that are available at home and capture 1 mesmerizing photograph based on a surprise theme.
